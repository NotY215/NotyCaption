1. INTRODUCTION
---------------
Welcome to NotyCaption Pro . This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our software. By installing and using NotyCaption Pro, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. DATA WE DO NOT COLLECT
-------------------------
We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit:

• Your audio or video files
• Your generated subtitles/captions
• Your personal identifiable information (name, email, address, phone number)
• Your IP address
• Your browsing history
• Your keystrokes or mouse movements
• Your system files or documents
• Your passwords or credentials
• Your payment information
• Any biometric data
• Any location data

3. LOCAL PROCESSING (NORMAL MODE)
----------------------------------
When you use Normal (Local) Mode:

• All processing happens ENTIRELY on your own computer
• NO data is sent to any external server
• NO internet connection is required (except for initial model download)
• Temporary files are stored in your chosen temp directory
• These temp files are deleted when you close the application (best-effort basis)

4. ONLINE MODE (GOOGLE COLAB INTEGRATION)
-----------------------------------------
When you choose to use Online Mode:

• You must explicitly log in with your Google account
• We request ONLY these permissions:
  - Create temporary folders in your Google Drive
  - Upload temporary audio files (deleted after processing)
  - Create temporary Google Colab notebooks (deleted after processing)
  - Download generated subtitle files

• We DO NOT:
  - Read any other files in your Google Drive
  - Store your Google credentials or tokens permanently
  - Share your Google account information with any third party
  - Access your Google Photos, Email, Contacts, or any other services

• Google may collect data according to THEIR privacy policy:
  - https://policies.google.com/privacy
  - https://colab.research.google.com/notebooks/privacy.ipynb
