1. Introduction

Welcome to NotyCaption Pro Web Version ("we", "our", "us", "NotY215"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our web application. By using NotyCaption Pro Web, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Important Note: This is a pure client-side web application. All processing happens in Google Colab via your browser. No backend servers are involved.

2. Data We DO NOT Collect

We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit:

  • ❌ Your audio files (they stay in your Google Drive)
  • ❌ Your generated subtitles/captions
  • ❌ Your personal identifiable information
  • ❌ Your IP address
  • ❌ Your browsing history
  • ❌ Your keystrokes or mouse movements
  • ❌ Any biometric or location data

3. Browser Storage (Cookies & LocalStorage)

Our web application stores the following data in your browser:

  • 📦 LocalStorage: Google OAuth tokens (encrypted), user preferences (language, words per line, output format)
  • 🍪 Session Storage: Temporary operation data during Colab processing

Important:

  • All data is stored ONLY in your browser
  • We have NO access to this data
  • You can clear this data at any time via browser settings
  • No tracking cookies or analytics are used

4. How NotyCaption Pro Web Works

The application follows a privacy-first architecture:

  1. Login: You authenticate with Google directly. Your credentials never pass through our servers.
  2. Audio Upload: Audio files are uploaded directly to YOUR Google Drive.
  3. Processing: Google Colab processes the file using Whisper/Spleeter.
  4. Result: Generated files are saved back to YOUR Google Drive and downloaded to your browser.
  5. Cleanup: Temporary files are deleted from Google Drive.

No data ever passes through our servers. Everything happens between your browser, Google Drive, and Google Colab.
